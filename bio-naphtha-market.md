<p><p>The bio naphtha market is divided into two primary types: light and heavy bio naphtha. Light bio naphtha is characterized by lower boiling points and is typically derived from renewable sources, making it suitable for applications like transportation fuels and petrochemical feedstocks. Heavy bio naphtha, on the other hand, has higher boiling points and is often utilized in industries such as heating and as a feedstock for refining processes. Both types cater to diverse energy needs and sustainability goals.</p></p>
