<p><p>The antivenom immunoglobulin market is primarily divided into two types: polyvalent and monovalent antivenoms. Polyvalent antivenom is effective against multiple venomous species, making it suitable for regions with diverse snake populations. Monovalent antivenom, on the other hand, targets a specific type of venom, providing a more focused treatment for bites from particular snakes. The choice between these types depends on factors like regional snake varieties, medical protocols, and the specific needs of healthcare providers in treating envenomation cases.</p></p>
