<p><p>The Online Attendance Tracking System market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility and scalability, allowing users seamless access from any internet-enabled device. They typically involve lower upfront costs and easier software updates. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed directly on local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, they often require significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making them suitable for organizations with specific needs.</p></p>
