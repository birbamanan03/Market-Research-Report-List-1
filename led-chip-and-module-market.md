<p><p>The LED chip and module market can be divided into three main types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chips have a conventional design with light emitted from the top, suitable for general lighting applications. Vertical chips provide enhanced thermal management and higher efficiency, making them ideal for high-power applications. Flip chips have a unique design where the chip is flipped upside down, enabling better heat dissipation and increasing luminous output, making them suitable for dense configurations and advanced lighting solutions.</p></p>
