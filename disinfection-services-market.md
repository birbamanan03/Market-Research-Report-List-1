<p><p>The disinfection services market is primarily categorized into physical and chemical methods. Physical methods include techniques such as ultraviolet (UV) light, heat, and filtration to eliminate pathogens without the use of chemicals. In contrast, chemical methods utilize various disinfectants like chlorine, hydrogen peroxide, and quaternary ammonium compounds to achieve microbial control. Both methods serve crucial roles in maintaining hygiene and safety in various settings, such as healthcare facilities, commercial spaces, and homes, ensuring effective pathogen management.</p></p>
