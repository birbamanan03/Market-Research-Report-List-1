<p><strong>The Synchronous Electric Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Excited Synchronous Electric Motors</li><li>Non-Excited Synchronous Electric Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synchronous electric motors market is primarily divided into two types: DC excited synchronous motors and non-excited synchronous motors. DC excited synchronous motors utilize an external DC source to provide excitation, ensuring effective torque production and operational stability. In contrast, non-excited synchronous motors, such as permanent magnet synchronous motors, rely on permanent magnets for excitation, offering higher efficiency and reduced maintenance. Both types are widely used in industrial applications, robotics, and renewable energy systems, supporting precise speed control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
