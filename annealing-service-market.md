<p><ul><li> Complete Annealing Service</li><li>Isothermal Annealing Service</li><li>Diffusion Annealing Service (Homogenizing)</li><li>Spheroidizing Annealing Service</li><li>Stress Relieving Annealing Service </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The annealing service market includes various specialized treatments to enhance material properties. Complete annealing service involves heating and cooling metals to relieve stresses and improve ductility. Isothermal annealing maintains a constant temperature to optimize microstructure. Diffusion annealing, or homogenizing, ensures uniformity in composition. Spheroidizing transforms hard steel into a more workable form for machining. Stress relieving annealing specifically targets residual stresses from manufacturing processes, improving material toughness and performance in applications. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs and requirements.</p></p>
