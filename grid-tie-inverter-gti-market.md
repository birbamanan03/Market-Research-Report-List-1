<p><p>A Grid-tie inverter (GTI) connects renewable energy systems, like solar panels, to the electrical grid, allowing for energy exchange. The market for GTIs includes two main types: low-frequency inverters, which use transformer-based designs for better performance in high-power applications, and high-frequency inverters, which employ transformerless technology for lighter, more compact units. Low-frequency inverters are suited for large installations, while high-frequency inverters are favored for residential or smaller setups due to their efficiency and size advantages.</p></p>
