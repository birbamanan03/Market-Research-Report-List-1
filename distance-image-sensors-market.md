<p><p>The distance image sensors market comprises two primary types: distance linear image sensors and distance area image sensors. Distance linear image sensors capture data along a single line, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspection and automated measurements. In contrast, distance area image sensors capture data over a two-dimensional area, allowing for more comprehensive spatial analysis, commonly used in robotics, security, and autonomous vehicles. Both types enhance object detection and distance measurement capabilities in various industries.</p></p>
