<p><p>Military Airborne Collision Avoidance Systems encompass various technologies aimed at preventing in-flight collisions. Radar systems provide real-time tracking of nearby aircraft. TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) alerts pilots to potential threats. PCAS (Passive Collision Avoidance System) enhances situational awareness without active transmissions. FLARM is designed for gliders and light aircraft. GPWS (Ground Proximity Warning System) and TAWS (Terrain Awareness and Warning System) warn pilots of obstacles and terrain. Synthetic Vision offers enhanced visual representations, while OCAS (Onboard Collision Avoidance System) integrates multiple data sources for comprehensive safety.</p></p>
