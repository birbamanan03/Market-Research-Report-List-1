<p><strong>The Hollow Polysulfone Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hollow Polysulfone Dialyzer market is primarily categorized into two types based on membrane flux: Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane. Low Flux Membranes are designed for patients requiring less intensive dialysis, allowing for the removal of small molecules and urea but minimizing water removal. In contrast, High Flux Membranes facilitate the efficient clearance of larger molecules and provide faster dialysis sessions, making them suitable for patients with more complex renal issues and those needing enhanced solute removal.</p></p>
