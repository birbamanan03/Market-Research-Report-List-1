<p><p>The spray drying tower market comprises various types of technology, each serving distinct applications. Centrifugal spray utilizes a rotating mechanism for consistent droplet formation, making it suitable for food and pharmaceuticals. Pressure spray operates by forcing liquid through a nozzle under pressure, ensuring fine particle size, commonly used in ceramics and chemicals. Chinese medicine spray drying focuses on preserving the active ingredients in herbal extracts, while air spray utilizes high-speed air to disperse liquids, ideal for producing powders from liquids like juices and dairy.</p></p>
