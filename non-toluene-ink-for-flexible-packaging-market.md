<p><p>Non-toluene inks for flexible packaging are gaining traction due to their environmentally friendly properties. Ethyl acetate, characterized by its fast evaporation rate and good adhesion, is often used for printing on non-porous substrates. N-butyl acetate, with a slower drying time, is preferred for applications requiring more extended workability and better flow. Both types are suitable for various flexible packaging materials, offering strong performance without the health and environmental concerns associated with traditional toluene-based inks.</p></p>
