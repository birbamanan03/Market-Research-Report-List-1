<p><p>Polyurethane Prepolymer (PPU) is a versatile material used in various applications, categorized primarily into two types: TDI (Toluene Diisocyanate) and MDI (Methylene Diphenyl Diisocyanate) polyurethane prepolymers. TDI prepolymers are typically used in flexible foams and coatings due to their excellent elasticity, while MDI prepolymers are favored for rigid foams and a variety of industrial applications due to their superior strength and stability. Other types may include specialty formulations designed for specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
