<p><p>Solar Electric Propulsion Systems utilize various technologies for propulsion in space missions. Hall Effect Thrusters (HET) are widely used for their high efficiency and thrust-to-power ratio, making them ideal for long-duration spacecraft operations. Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT) offer a simpler design with lower thrust and are suitable for smaller spacecraft or technical demonstrations. Other propulsion systems include ion engines and arcjet systems, each contributing uniquely to the market by addressing specific mission requirements and operational constraints.</p></p>
