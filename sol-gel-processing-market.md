<p><p>The Sol-Gel Processing market includes various coating methods such as rotational coating, immersion coating, and roll coating. Rotational coating involves spinning a substrate to achieve an even layer of sol-gel material. Immersion coating submerges the substrate in a sol-gel solution, allowing uniform coverage. Roll coating uses a roller to apply the sol-gel solution, ideal for continuous processes. Other methods may include spray coating and dip coating, broadening the market's diverse applications in sectors like electronics, optics, and materials science.</p></p>
