<p><p>The industrial denitration catalyst market is primarily divided into two types: cellular and flat catalysts. Cellular catalysts feature a honeycomb structure, providing a larger surface area for effective gas flow and reaction, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Flat catalysts, on the other hand, have a more straightforward design and are often used in applications with limited space. Both types play crucial roles in reducing nitrogen oxides emissions, thereby enhancing environmental compliance in various industrial processes.</p></p>
