Manual diesel nozzles require operators to control the fuel dispensing process, allowing for customized fuel flow. In contrast, automatic diesel nozzles feature sensors that detect fuel levels and automatically shut off when the tank is full, enhancing safety and preventing spills. Both types serve critical roles in fuel efficiency and precision delivery, catering to various applications in transportation and industrial sectors.</p></p>
