<p><strong>The Insect Growth Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors</li><li>Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics</li><li>Anti-juvenile Hormone Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Insect Growth Regulator (IGR) market consists of three main types: Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors, which disrupt the formation of chitin, essential for insect exoskeleton development; Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics, designed to simulate juvenile hormones, preventing insects from maturing; and Anti-juvenile Hormone Agents, which inhibit the action of juvenile hormones, leading to premature development. These IGRs are crucial in pest management, effectively controlling insect populations by targeting their growth processes without harming beneficial species.</p></p>
