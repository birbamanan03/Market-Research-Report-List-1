<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber cable market primarily consists of two types: single-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single-mode fiber features a thin core allowing only one light mode to propagate, resulting in higher bandwidth and longer distance transmission, ideal for telecommunication networks. In contrast, multimode fiber has a larger core that permits multiple light modes, making it suitable for short-distance applications like local area networks. Both types cater to diverse communication needs, supporting advancements in data transfer and connectivity.</p></p>
