<p><p>The **Special Film Market** includes diverse types tailored for specific applications. **Stretch Film** is elastic, used for wrapping and securing pallets. **Shrink Film** contracts under heat, ideal for packaging and bundling. **Barrier Film** protects against moisture, gases, or UV, ensuring product longevity. **Conductive Film** enables static dissipation or EMI shielding in electronics. **Safety & Security Film** strengthens glass, preventing break-ins or shattering. **Anti-Fog Film** prevents condensation, enhancing visibility. **Other Films** cater to niche needs like UV protection, insulation, or decorative purposes. Each type serves unique industrial or consumer demands.</p></p>
