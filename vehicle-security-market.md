<p><p>The vehicle security market encompasses various systems designed to protect automobiles from theft and unauthorized access. Alarm systems alert owners of potential theft, while immobilizers disable the engine, preventing unauthorized use. Remote keyless entry allows drivers to unlock doors and start engines without physical keys, enhancing convenience. Passive keyless entry enhances security by automatically unlocking doors when the authorized key fob approaches. Central locking systems allow drivers to lock or unlock all doors simultaneously, improving safety and ease of access.</p></p>
