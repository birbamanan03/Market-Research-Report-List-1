<p><p>The hardwood floor cleaner market is categorized into three main types: water cleaners, semi-aqueous cleaners, and non-aqueous cleaners. Water cleaners primarily utilize water as the solvent, making them easy to use and eco-friendly. Semi-aqueous cleaners combine water with mild solvents, offering stronger cleaning power while remaining relatively safe for the environment. Non-aqueous cleaners employ organic solvents, providing robust cleaning capabilities for tough stains but may require more caution due to their chemical composition. Each type caters to different cleaning needs and preferences.</p></p>
