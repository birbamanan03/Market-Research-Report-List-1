<p><ul><li>Flexible Esophagoscope</li><li>Transnasal Esophagoscope</li><li>Rigid Esophagoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The esophagoscopy instrument market comprises three main types: flexible esophagoscopes, transnasal esophagoscopes, and rigid esophagoscopes. Flexible esophagoscopes are most commonly used due to their ability to navigate the esophagus easily, providing high-resolution images for diagnosis and treatment. Transnasal esophagoscopes are designed for minimally invasive access via the nose, enhancing patient comfort. Rigid esophagoscopes, while less flexible, offer stability and are typically used in surgical procedures, enabling direct visualization and intervention in the esophagus. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and patient scenarios.</p></p>
