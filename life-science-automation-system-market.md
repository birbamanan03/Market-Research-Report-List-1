<p><p>The Life Science Automation System market is primarily divided into Total Laboratory Automation (TLA) and Separate Pre-treatment Systems (TTA). TLA integrates complete laboratory workflows, streamlining processes from specimen processing to analysis, enhancing efficiency and throughput. In contrast, TTA focuses on individual pre-treatment phases, automating initial tasks before further analysis, allowing for flexibility in laboratory setups. Both systems aim to improve productivity, accuracy, and reliability in laboratory operations, catering to the evolving needs of the life sciences sector.</p></p>
