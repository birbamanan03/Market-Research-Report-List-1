<p><ul><li>Invasive Glucose Sensor</li><li>Non-Invasive Glucose Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin pump sensor market comprises two primary types: invasive glucose sensors and non-invasive glucose sensors. Invasive glucose sensors require a small device to be implanted under the skin to measure glucose levels directly from interstitial fluid, providing accurate real-time data. Non-invasive glucose sensors, on the other hand, estimate glucose levels through external means, such as optical, thermal, or electromagnetic methods, without skin penetration. Each type offers distinct benefits, influencing user choice based on convenience, comfort, and accuracy.</p></p>
