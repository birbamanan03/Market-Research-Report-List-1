<p><strong>The Airlaid Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Latex Bonded Airlaid (LBAL)</li><li>Thermal Bonded Airlaid (TBAL)</li><li>Multi Bonded Airlaid (MBAL)</li><li>Hydrogen Bonded Airlaid (HBAL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airlaid products market includes several types based on bonding methods. Latex Bonded Airlaid (LBAL) uses latex as a binder, offering flexibility and softness. Thermal Bonded Airlaid (TBAL) employs heat to bond fibers, enhancing durability and strength. Multi Bonded Airlaid (MBAL) integrates various binding techniques for improved performance and versatility. Hydrogen Bonded Airlaid (HBAL) utilizes hydrogen bonds, providing lightweight structure and absorption properties. Each type caters to specific applications in hygiene, household, and industrial sectors, emphasizing unique benefits according to bonding methods.</p></p>
