<p><strong>The Motorized Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Type</li><li>Piezoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorized positioners market is primarily divided into electric type and piezoelectric type. Electric type positioners utilize electromagnetic motors to achieve precise motion control, ideal for applications requiring high torque and speed. These are commonly used in robotics and automation. In contrast, piezoelectric type positioners employ piezoelectric materials that expand or contract when voltage is applied, providing exceptional accuracy and quick responses. These are preferred in applications requiring nanometer-level precision, such as optical systems and micro-manipulation tasks.</p></p>
