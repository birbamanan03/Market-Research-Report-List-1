<p><p>The bath lift market consists of two primary types: automatic bath lifts and semi-automatic bath lifts. Automatic bath lifts are fully powered devices that allow users to effortlessly lower and raise themselves into and out of the bathtub with the push of a button, offering convenience and ease of use. Semi-automatic bath lifts require some manual assistance from the user, often involving hand-cranking or adjusting features, but still provide essential support for those with mobility challenges, enhancing safety and independence in bathing.</p></p>
