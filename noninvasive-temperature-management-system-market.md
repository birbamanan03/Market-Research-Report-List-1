<p><p>The Noninvasive Temperature Management System market is segmented into two primary types: Patient Cooling Systems and Patient Warming Systems. Patient Cooling Systems are designed to lower body temperature safely, often used in critical care or during surgeries to prevent neurological damage. Conversely, Patient Warming Systems provide controlled heat to raise body temperature, essential in preventing hypothermia during procedures or in recovery. Both systems enhance patient outcomes by maintaining optimal thermal comfort and stability during medical interventions.</p></p>
