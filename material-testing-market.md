<p><p>The material testing market is categorized into several types, including Universal Testing Machines (UTMs), Servohydraulic Testing Machines, and hardness testing equipment. UTMs are versatile devices used for tensile, compression, and bending tests across various materials. Servohydraulic machines provide precise control for dynamic testing, ideal for large-scale components. Hardness testing equipment measures material resistance to deformation, crucial for quality assurance. Together, these technologies ensure material reliability, performance characterization, and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
