<p><p>The Anticoagulant Therapeutic Drug Monitoring Assay Kit market is segmented into three main types: Fluorescence Polarization Immunoassay (FPIA), Homogeneous Enzyme Immunoassay (HEIA), and Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA). FPIA utilizes fluorescent labels to measure drug levels, offering rapid results. HEIA employs enzymes in a single-phase system, allowing quick and efficient analysis. ELISA, a more traditional approach, uses a solid-phase format and multiple steps to detect and quantify drug concentration, providing sensitivity and specificity for therapeutic monitoring.</p></p>
