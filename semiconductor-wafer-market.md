<p><p>The semiconductor wafer market is divided into two main types: Front-End-of-Line (FEOL) and Back-End-of-Line (BEOL). FEOL involves the initial processes of wafer fabrication, including the deposition and etching of materials to create transistors and circuit elements. BEOL refers to the latter stages, where interconnections, packaging, and assembly occur to form complete semiconductor devices. Together, these processes are essential for producing efficient and functional microelectronics, impacting various sectors like consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications.</p></p>
