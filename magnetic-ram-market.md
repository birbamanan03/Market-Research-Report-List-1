<p><strong>The Magnetic RAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic RAM market primarily consists of two types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic field changes to switch memory cells, offering high speed and endurance suitable for applications like embedded systems and consumer electronics. In contrast, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) leverages spin torque effects to switch states, enabling higher density and lower power consumption, making it ideal for advanced computing and storage solutions. Both technologies aim to enhance performance and reliability in non-volatile memory applications.</p></p>
