<p><p>The animal parasiticides market comprises three main types: endoparasiticides, ectoparasiticides, and endectocides. Endoparasiticides target internal parasites like worms and protozoa, effectively safeguarding the gastrointestinal health of livestock and pets. Ectoparasiticides focus on external parasites such as fleas, ticks, and mites, providing crucial protection against infestations on the skin and fur. Endectocides combine both effects, targeting internal and external parasites simultaneously, offering comprehensive control for various animal species, including agricultural and companion animals.</p></p>
