<p><strong>The Make-up Air Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Gas Supplement</li><li>Indirect Gas Supplement</li><li>Non-gas Supplement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Make-up Air Units (MAUs) are crucial for maintaining indoor air quality by replenishing exhausted air. The market is segmented into three types: </p><p>1. **Direct Gas Supplement** MAUs use direct-fired gas burners to heat incoming air, ensuring quick temperature adjustments. </p><p>2. **Indirect Gas Supplement** units utilize a heat exchanger, providing efficient air heating while avoiding combustion gases in the airstream.</p><p>3. **Non-gas Supplement** systems rely on electric or hydronic heat methods, catering to applications where gas is unavailable or undesirable. Each type serves specific operational needs and energy efficiency goals.</p></p>
