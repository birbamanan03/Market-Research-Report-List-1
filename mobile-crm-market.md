<p><p>The Mobile CRM market is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise CRM involves software installed directly on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but requiring extensive IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CRM is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, facilitating access from various devices. This model allows businesses to leverage real-time updates and collaborative features without heavy infrastructure investment.</p></p>
