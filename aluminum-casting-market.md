<p><p>Aluminum casting encompasses various techniques, each suited for specific applications. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into molds for precise, complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Permanent molding casting uses reusable metal molds, allowing for efficient production of medium volumes with good dimensional accuracy. Sand casting employs sand molds, providing versatility for large or intricate parts but generally lower precision. Other methods include shell casting and investment casting, catering to specialized requirements across diverse industries.</p></p>
