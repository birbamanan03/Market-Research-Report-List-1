<p><ul><li>Linear Piezo Stages</li><li>Rotary Piezo Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo positioners market is categorized into linear and rotary piezo stages. Linear piezo stages enable precise movement along a straight path, commonly used in applications such as microscopy, optical alignment, and semiconductor manufacturing. Rotary piezo stages facilitate controlled rotational movements, essential for tasks requiring angular positioning, such as beam steering and component manipulation. Both types leverage piezoelectric technology for high-resolution positioning, rapid response times, and stability, making them crucial in fields like research, automation, and instrumentation.</p></p>
