<p><p>Aluminium Foam Sandwich (AFS) refers to composite materials made of layers of aluminium foam sandwiched between two aluminium plates, offering lightweight, high strength, and excellent energy absorption. The market includes two types: Ex-situ Bonded AFS, where the foam is created separately and then bonded to the metal layers, enhancing design flexibility; and In-situ Bonded AFS, where the foam is formed during the bonding process, resulting in improved structural integrity and bond strength. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
