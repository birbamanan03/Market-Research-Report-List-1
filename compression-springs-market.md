<p><strong>The Compression Springs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conical</li><li>Hourglass</li><li>Barrel-shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression springs come in various shapes, each serving specific applications in the market. Conical springs taper at both ends, providing a progressive resistance ideal for variable load conditions. Hourglass springs feature a narrower center, allowing for stability and space-saving design. Barrel-shaped springs have a uniform diameter, enabling consistent compression and force distribution, making them suitable for heavy loads. The choice between these types depends on the application requirements, including space constraints, load types, and desired spring characteristics.</p></p>
