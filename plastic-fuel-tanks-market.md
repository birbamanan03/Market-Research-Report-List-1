<p><p>The plastic fuel tanks market is segmented into single-layer and multilayer types. Single-layer plastic fuel tanks are made from a single material layer, offering basic fuel containment primarily used in low-cost applications. In contrast, multilayer plastic fuel tanks feature multiple layers, providing enhanced durability, resistance to permeation, and chemical protection, making them suitable for high-performance and environmentally sensitive applications. These multilayer tanks are increasingly favored for their ability to meet stringent regulatory standards and improve fuel efficiency.</p></p>
