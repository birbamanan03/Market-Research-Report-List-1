<p><p>The ion-exchange resin market primarily comprises two types: cation exchange resins and anion exchange resins. Cation exchange resins are designed to remove positively charged ions from solutions, often used in water softening and purification. Anion exchange resins, on the other hand, target negatively charged ions, effectively extracting contaminants such as nitrates and phosphates. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, enabling efficient ion removal and enhancing product quality.</p></p>
