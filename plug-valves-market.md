<p><p>The plug valves market consists of several types catering to different operational needs. Non-lubricated plug valves operate without additional lubrication, ideal for various fluids. Lubricated plug valves include internal lubricant to minimize wear and enhance sealing. Eccentric plug valves feature an off-center design that allows for smoother operation and reduced friction. Expanding plug valves utilize an adjustable mechanism to enhance sealing and accommodate wear, making them suitable for demanding applications. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
