<p><strong>The Quartz Glass Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Free</li><li>High Alkali</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz glass fibers are categorized primarily into two types: alkali-free and high alkali. Alkali-free fibers contain minimal alkaline metal oxides, offering superior thermal resistance and chemical durability, making them suitable for demanding applications such as aerospace and electronics. High alkali fibers, on the other hand, contain higher levels of alkaline components, which enhance flexibility and ease of processing. These fibers are typically used in applications requiring lower thermal resistance and where cost-effectiveness is prioritized, like insulation and construction.</p></p>
