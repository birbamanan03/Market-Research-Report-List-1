<p><p>Advanced Power Modules for the industrial market encompass Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs combine power semiconductor devices with integrated control circuitry, enhancing efficiency in motor drives and power conversion applications. PIMs, on the other hand, integrate multiple power components, including drivers and thermal management systems, into a single module, optimizing performance, size, and reliability. Both technologies support the demand for compact, high-performance solutions in automation, robotics, and renewable energy systems, driving innovation in industrial applications.</p></p>
