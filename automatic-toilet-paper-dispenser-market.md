<p><p>The automatic toilet paper dispenser market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact dispensers. Contact toilet paper dispensers require users to touch a button or lever to dispense paper, which may pose hygiene concerns. In contrast, non-contact toilet paper dispensers utilize sensors to automatically provide paper, promoting a cleaner and more hygienic user experience. Both types cater to various settings, such as public restrooms and commercial facilities, with the non-contact variant gaining popularity for its germ-reduction benefits.</p></p>
